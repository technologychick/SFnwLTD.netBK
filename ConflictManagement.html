Level 2 Award for Conflict Management

The level 2 Award in Conflict Management has been developed to meet the requirements of individuals who require training in conflict management. It is appropriate for a wide range of sectors and is suitable for anyone who has a customer facing role, deals with service users or the public. Price - £100.00

For course dates, enquiries or more information please click here
Course Content The course consist of one units. The units are outlined below:
• Unit 1 – Managing conflict in the workplace when dealing with Customers, Service Users or the Public
1 day (14 Guided Learning Hours)

Entry Requirements The qualification is aimed at learners aged 16 and over. Although no formal qualifications are required to take the course, it is advised that learners have a minimum of Level 1 in literacy or numeracy or equivalent. You must also be able to provide personal photographic identification and proof of address – details will be sent to you in your welcome pack. Learners must also posses a good understanding of English
