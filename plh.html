Level 2 Award for Personal Licence Holders

Highfield ABC Level 2 Award for Personal License Holder.
Guided learning hours
We recommend that the following guided learning hours (GLHs) provide a suitable course length for each unit.

Unit 1 – Legal and Social Responsibilities of a Personal Licence Holder 8 hours

Guided learning hours include direct contact hours as well as other times when candidates are completing work that has been agreed with teachers or training providers. It is the responsibility of training centres to decide the appropriate course duration based on their candidates’ ability and level of existing knowledge. Highfield ABC experience indicates that the number of GLHs can vary significantly from one training centre to another according to candidates' needs.

Structure of the qualification

Highfield ABC L2 Award for Personal License Holder consists of the following units and elements:

1. Legal and Social Responsibilities of a Personal Licence Holder

2. Understand the nature, purpose and period of validity of a personal licence

3. Understand the application process and legal duties when applying for a personal licence.

4. Understand the legal duties of a personal licence

5. Understand the roles, responsibilities and functions of licensing authorities

6. Understand the licensing objectives and the importance of partnerships in promoting these objectives.

7. Understand the nature and strength of alcohol and the effect on the body

8. Understand the law in relation to premises licences

9. Understand the role and responsibilities of the Designated Premises Supervisor (DPS)

10. Understand the law in relation to Temporary Event Notices

11. Understand the rights and legal duties of the responsible persons in relation to operation of licensed promises

12. Understand the powers of the police and other authorities in relation to licensed premises

13. Understand prohibitions and exemptions in relation to licensable activities
