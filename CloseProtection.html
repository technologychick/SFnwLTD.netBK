Close Protection Services

SSNW Ltd provides the highest possible standard for a professional, reliable and discreet protective service. Our Operatives are personally selected and tailored to the exact requirements of our clients. We provide both experienced male and female trained Close Protection Officers. All of which have extensive experience from within the security industry. All our Close Protection Officers are SIA licensed, vetted and are highly experienced.
Whatever your business or lifestyle, SSNW Ltd have a solution for you. All of our services can be provided throughout the UK as a single service or as part of a tailored package.
