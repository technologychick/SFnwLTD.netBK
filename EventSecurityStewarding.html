Stewarding and Event Security Services


Safety is paramount at any event and here at SFNW Ltd it is our job to ensure your event runs smoothly.

All our staff under go training in The NVQ L2 and NVQ L3 Spectator Safety and continue to undergo further training throughout their time with ourselves. Therefore you can rest assured that you will be getting well trained staff who can work well under instruction and can deliver excellent service.
